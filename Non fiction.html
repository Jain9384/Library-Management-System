Discover the Power of Non-Fiction

Non-fiction books inform, educate, and inspire by presenting real-world knowledge, facts, and true stories. Whether you're interested in science, self-development, biographies, or current events, non-fiction helps expand your understanding of the world around you.

1. Educated by Tara Westover

A memoir of a woman who escapes her survivalist upbringing to pursue education, ultimately earning a PhD from Cambridge.

2. Atomic Habits by James Clear

A practical guide to building good habits, breaking bad ones, and mastering the tiny behaviors that lead to remarkable results.

3. Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman

An exploration of how we think — the fast, intuitive way and the slow, logical way — and how these systems shape our decisions.

4. Becoming by Michelle Obama

An intimate and powerful memoir from the former First Lady of the United States, sharing her personal story and life journey.

5. The Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle

A spiritual classic that teaches the importance of living in the present moment to find peace and purpose.

Why Read Non-Fiction?

  • Gain real-world knowledge and skills
  • Learn from true stories and experiences
  • Develop better critical thinking and awareness
  • Stay informed about history, science, and culture
  • Get inspired by extraordinary lives and ideas
