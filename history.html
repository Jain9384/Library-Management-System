Explore Our History Collection

History allows us to understand the world by reflecting on the events, people, and cultures that shaped it. Dive into our curated selection of influential and engaging history books — from ancient civilizations to modern times.

1. Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind by Yuval Noah Harari

Explore how Homo sapiens became the dominant species on Earth through cognitive, agricultural, and scientific revolutions.

2. Guns, Germs, and Steel by Jared Diamond

This book explains how geography, agriculture, and innovation shaped civilizations — not race or culture.

3. A People's History of the United States by Howard Zinn

A retelling of American history from the viewpoint of marginalized groups like workers, women, and minorities.

4. The Silk Roads: A New History of the World by Peter Frankopan

A fascinating journey through the East's historical significance via trade, culture, and diplomacy.

5. The Diary of a Young Girl by Anne Frank

A deeply moving personal account of life in hiding during the Holocaust, offering a powerful perspective on World War II.

Why Read History?

  • Understand patterns that repeat over time
  • Learn from past mistakes and successes
  • Appreciate cultural diversity and heritage
  • Foster critical thinking and global awareness
