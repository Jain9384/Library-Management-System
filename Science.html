Explore Our Science Collection

Science opens up the mysteries of the universe, empowering us to understand the natural world, innovate, and solve complex problems. Dive into our curated collection of thought-provoking science books that span physics, biology, astronomy, and beyond.

1. A Brief History of Time by Stephen Hawking

Explore profound questions about the universe — from the Big Bang to black holes — in this groundbreaking classic by one of the most brilliant physicists of our time.

2. The Selfish Gene by Richard Dawkins

A revolutionary perspective on evolution, this book introduces the gene-centered view of natural selection with clarity and imagination.

3. The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks by Rebecca Skloot

This compelling story blends science, ethics, and race, telling how one woman’s cells changed medical research forever.

4. Astrophysics for People in a Hurry by Neil deGrasse Tyson

A witty and accessible introduction to cosmic science, ideal for busy readers who want to understand the universe.

5. The Gene: An Intimate History by Siddhartha Mukherjee

A sweeping narrative that explores the history and science of genetic discovery, interwoven with personal and human stories.

Why Read Science?

  • Fuel curiosity about how things work
  • Understand the universe and life on Earth
  • Build critical thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Appreciate scientific contributions to modern society
  • Stay informed about technological and medical advances
