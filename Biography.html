Discover Remarkable Lives with Biographies

Biography books offer powerful insights into the lives of influential people — their struggles, achievements, and the impact they made on the world. From historical icons to modern-day changemakers, these stories inspire, educate, and empower.

1. The Diary of a Young Girl by Anne Frank

An unforgettable account of a young girl's life in hiding during the Holocaust, revealing the human spirit’s strength in dark times.

2. Long Walk to Freedom by Nelson Mandela

The moving autobiography of South Africa’s first Black president — a tale of resilience, leadership, and the fight against apartheid.

3. Becoming by Michelle Obama

An intimate, powerful, and inspiring memoir by the former First Lady of the United States, reflecting on her life, values, and voice.

4. Steve Jobs by Walter Isaacson

Based on exclusive interviews, this biography dives into the innovation, creativity, and complexity of the Apple co-founder.

5. Wings of Fire by A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

An inspiring story of India’s beloved scientist and former President, chronicling his journey from humble beginnings to national hero.

Why Read Biographies?

  • Learn from the experiences and choices of influential people
  • Gain insight into different cultures, eras, and fields
  • Get inspired by stories of resilience, leadership, and purpose
  • Understand the human side of history, politics, science, and art
  • Reflect on your own values, goals, and journey through others' lives
